Dear Invited Guests & Graduates,

Congratulations on reaching this important milestone in your academic journey. We are delighted to invite you to the University Convocation ceremony, where we will celebrate your achievements and mark the beginning of your new chapter.

Convocation Awards.

The following is the description of the awards that are to be presented today.It contains the name of the award and the criterias to which it is given to a specified graduand.These awards include;

A: The Rector's Award.

Awards given to students with the highest GPA amongst the graduands.

The prizes include a Crystal prize, Certificate and Cash.

B: NBS AWARD

An award to the student highest GPA amongst the graduands in the Masters of Offficial Statistics and Masters of Science in Agricultural Statistics .

C: OCGS AWARD

An award to the student highest GPA amongst the graduandsin the Bachelor Degree in Offficial Statistics, Data Science, Agricultural Statistics and Economics and Business Statistics and Economics.

D: Data Vision Award.

An award to the student highest GPA amongst the graduands in Ordinary Diploma in Official Statistics.

E: SENATE AWARD.

Appreciation Certificates from the Senate to the Students with Distinction(for postgraduates) and First class (for undergraduates).

Academic Staff's Awards.

Awards like Godfrey Saga's award, Leguma Bakari's award, Dr. Siamrie Lyro's award and Edwin Magoti's award given to students with outstanding credibility in various aspects of statistics.

The Awards are interms of Cash.

Certificates of Merits to EASTC-SO Leaders.

Awarded to the leadership of the Student Organization for their Accountability in the Administrative tasks presented to them by the Centre.

The Award will be a Certificate for Each Leader.
